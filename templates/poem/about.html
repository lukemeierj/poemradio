About

Why Does This Exist

Welcome to PoemRad.io! I built this website to help amateur poets showcase their work somewhere that makes it easy for poetry lovers to sit back and enjoy what they read. Our mission is poetry without distractions. No ads. No popups. No sidebar suggestions. Just sit back and let us show you some stellar poetry. There are other fantastic websites for sharing and viewing poetry if you want a more forum/social network feelâ€”I personally recomment Eliot's Hello Poetry.

What Makes This Special

Reading poetry should be as pleasant as the poetry itself, so I've tried to keep the website as simple as possible. Also, whenever you like or dislike a poem, we take note and help show you the poems you want to read. It's like Pandora with songs, Amazon with products, or Facebook with your friends' cat photos.
